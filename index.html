Seamless Installation, Perfect Finish

From first measurements to final fitting, every surface is handled with precision and care.

At Sabre Stone Commercial, we offer full-service installation for all our products from architectural wall cladding and sculptural tables to bespoke worktops and custom basins. Every step is carefully managed to ensure a seamless, high-performance result that elevates your project.

  • Templating – Precision from the Start
    Our specialist surveyors conduct detailed site measurements using millimetre-accurate tools, planning layout, cut-outs, and joins to ensure visual flow and optimal performance.
  • Fabrication – Engineered in Surrey
    All components are crafted in our Surrey facility using CNC precision and expert hand-finishing to achieve a true made-to-measure fit.
  • Installation – The Perfect Fit
    Our installation teams deliver a clean, disruption-free service, aligning every slab with flawless joins for a refined, seamless finish.
  • Millimetre-accurate templating to final fit
  • Premium Laminam porcelain sourced from Italy
  • In-house fabrication in Surrey
  • Minimal joins for a seamless aesthetic
  • Full project management with zero compromise
