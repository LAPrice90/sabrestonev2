Seamless Installation, Perfect Finish

From first measurements to final fitting, every surface is handled with precision and care.

At Sabre Stone Commercial, we offer full-service installation for all our products — from architectural wall cladding and sculptural tables to bespoke worktops and custom basins. Every step is carefully managed to ensure a seamless, high-performance result.

  • Templating – Precision from the Start
    We begin with a detailed site survey, using millimetre-accurate tools to plan layout, cut-outs, and joins — ensuring optimal flow and visual balance.
  • Fabrication – Engineered in Surrey
    All components are custom-fabricated in our Surrey facility, combining CNC accuracy with expert hand-finishing for a made-to-measure fit.
  • Installation – The Perfect Fit
    Our specialist teams deliver a smooth, disruption-free install, aligning every detail with clean joins and a flawless finish.

Cladding Example: For a 2800mm x 6000mm wall, we fabricate four 1500mm x 2800mm slabs with all fixture cut-outs pre-planned. The result? A join-free aesthetic and a true bespoke finish.

  • Premium Italian porcelain from Laminam
  • In-house fabrication in Surrey
  • Minimal joins across all installations
  • Seamless project execution with zero compromise
