PRODUCT RANGE

Wall Cladding

Architectural Cladding, Seamlessly Delivered

Our wall cladding solutions combine large-format Italian porcelain with exacting fabrication for clean, modern installations. Every panel is templated, cut, and aligned to enhance flow while minimising visible joins.

Suitable for both interiors and exteriors, these high-performance surfaces deliver contemporary texture and architectural clarity ideal for lobbies, corridors, stairwells and façade systems.

Flooring

Engineered Surfaces That Endure

Our flooring solutions blend beauty and strength for high-traffic commercial environments. Using scratch-resistant, non-porous porcelain and stone, we create hard-wearing floors that retain their elegance under pressure.

From polished lobby spaces to retail rollouts and spa environments, our flooring is fabricated for precision fit, visual continuity, and long-term resilience.

Kitchen Worktops

Made-to-Measure Surfaces, Crafted to Last

Our kitchen worktops are fabricated with precision from the highest-grade stone and porcelain. Scratch, heat, and stain-resistant, each surface is built for longevity and finished to complement its architectural context.

We template, cut, and finish every piece in-house ensuring seamless fits, minimal joins, and a refined result across hospitality suites, show kitchens, and high-performance residential settings.

Furnishing Elements

Surfaces That Shape the Space

We craft bespoke furnishing elements shelving, counters, reception desks from porcelain and natural stone, offering form and function in equal measure. Each piece is fabricated to meet the spatial and stylistic needs of the setting.

From hospitality lobbies to retail fit-outs, these elements deliver tactile richness and visual structure, designed to endure in dynamic environments.

Exterior Façade

Lightweight Panels. Heavyweight Performance.

Our exterior cladding solutions use UV-stable, large-format porcelain to deliver a sleek, modern façade that meets both technical and aesthetic demands. These panels are engineered to resist weathering, staining, and thermal stress.

Precision-cut and mechanically mounted, each panel adds texture and identity to commercial buildings, public architecture, and upscale residential developments.

Washbasins & Vanities

Functional Elegance, Seamlessly Delivered

Our washbasins and vanities are sculpted from solid stone or porcelain slabs, designed as single pieces or integrated countertops. These surfaces are engineered to resist moisture, staining, and daily wear without sacrificing elegance.

Perfect for boutique hotels, spas, and luxury residences, each basin is made to measure and fabricated for easy installation and long-term performance.

Shower Trays

Flush Finishes. Built-In Elegance.

Our custom porcelain shower trays are fabricated as single-piece surfaces to deliver sleek, slip-resistant wet rooms with no visible joins. Their non-porous construction resists limescale, staining, and cosmetic products.

Trays are colour-matched to surrounding wall panels for a unified look, and can be fully recessed to align flush with surrounding floor finishes.

Cabinet Fronts
